Keyboard shortcuts allow you to quickly duplicate text or files.
These combinations work in almost all programs and make working on the computer much easier. You can also use them to easily insert commands into the console.

## Copy

- **Windows/Linux:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Ctrl+C" />

- **macOS:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Cmd+C" /> or <Kbd windows="⌘" />
    <Kbd windows="C" />

## Paste

- **Windows/Linux:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Ctrl+V" />

- **macOS:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Cmd+V" /> or <Kbd windows="⌘" />
    <Kbd windows="V" />

## Cut

- **Windows/Linux:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Ctrl+X" />

- **macOS:**
  - Press <Kbd windows="Cmd+X" /> or <Kbd windows="⌘" />
    <Kbd windows="X" />
