## Instructions

<Steps>
  0. If you are no longer connected via SSH, reconnect and enter your password. **Replace 'user' and 'hostname'** with those of your Raspberry Pi.
</Steps>

<div class="step-code">

```sh
ssh user@hostname.local
```

</div>

<Steps>

1. Use this command to **start the BirdNET-Pi installation**.
  The process may take around an hour, depending on your internet connection and system performance.
  Leave the terminal open until the installation is complete.

</Steps>

<div class="step-code">
```sh
curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Nachtzuster/BirdNET-Pi/main/newinstaller.sh | bash
```
</div>
